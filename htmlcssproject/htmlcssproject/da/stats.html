Hate Crime Statistics

What type of hate crime is most committed?

Reportedly, 54.3% targeted property and 45.7% targeted people

However, while hate crimes against property are greater than hate crimes against people, this is only temporary. Recently, hate crimes against individuals increased by 6.2% while hate crimes against property decreased significantly.

Most affected race for hate crime

Which race commits the most hate crimes?

According to a study of 6780 known offenders, 55.1% were white, 21.2% were Black or African American, and 15.7% other.

How old are hate crime offenders?

Of 6264 hate crime offenders, 89% were 18 years of age or older
