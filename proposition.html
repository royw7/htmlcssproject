Propositions to Reduce/Prevent Hate Crime


So how can this be prevented?

  1. Increase the punishment for hate crimes. Hate crimes are only either a Class 3 or 2 felony even if committed on multiple occassions.
  2. Create a special task force/strengthen officer's ability to prevent hate crime.
  3. Raise awareness of how to respond to hate crime.
  4. Equip minorities with self-defense tools.
  5. Video cameras should be installed and on at all times.

How are people getting away with hate crime?

There are too many occurences of hate crime and too little resistance to prevent it. Through police officers and self-defence tools/martial arts, most instances can be nipped in the bud before they become too large of an issue to handle. And if it does go past that, surveillance should be around every corner to document and record the event.
